

# Tim's Thoughts on "AI" as in Generative/LLM etc...

It seems pretty and impressive on what it can do. 

## How I Use it:
- As a brainstorming device when looking for a word/name/thought.
- As an advanced google, the thing is amazing at giving keywords you don't yet know.

## Why I don't Use it more:
- I enjoy the problem solving and writing of code; why take the fun part away.
- I tried using it 2024-2025 and it was making me very lazy; anything that required a small effort was like "I don't wanna do it"
